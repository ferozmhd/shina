MALIKS FAMILY

Arman malik

Armaan Malik(born 22 July 1995)is an Indian singer songwriter, record producer, voice-over, performer and actor. He is the younger son of Daboo Malik and Jyothi Malik, and grandson of Sardar Malik.

Amaal malik

Amaal Mallik is an Indian music director, composer, singer, performer and lyricist. He is the elder son of Daboo Malik and Jyothi Malik, and grandson of Sardar Malik. He is brother of singer Armaan Malik and nephew of music composer and singer Anu Malik.

Daboo malik

Daboo Malik is an Indian music director, composer, singer, actor and scriptwriter in the Indian Music Industry. Daboo Malik was born in Bombay, Maharashtra to the veteran music director Sardar Malik.

Anu malik

Anu Malik (born 2 November 1960) is an Indian music director and singer. He is an Indian National Award and Filmfare Award winning music director, who primarily composes music for the Hindi film industry. He is the son of Sardar Malik.
